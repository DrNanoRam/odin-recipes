Ramen

Description

If you are anything like me, you love the tast of top ramen. The problem though is that it isn't particularly healthy. Here is a way to add to it to make it at least a little more nutritious.

Ingredients

  • Top Ramen. Pick whatever flavor of combination of flavors you enjoy.
  • Veggies. It is easiest to get a mixed back of frozen veggies at the store.
  • Butter
  • Any meats you want
  • Eggs if you want.
  • Spices such as salt, pepper, onion powder, or even a mixed blend such as Mrs. Dash.

Steps

  1. Cut up and cook your meats in a skillet to the appropriate temperature.
  2. Add butter to a pan and cook your veggies.
  3. Boil an egg for 5-10 minutes, depending on how hard you want it. Afterwards, run the egg under cold water and remove the shell.
  4. Cook your packet of ramen per the instructions on bag. Aftwards, add all your other cooked ingredients and any spices.
  5. Enjoy!
